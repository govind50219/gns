Table Tennis


TT aka table tennis, the fastest ball game and an integral part of IITK culture . This game is about tremendous reflexes .It is the most popular game in campus and played 24x7. Practically everyone here had fascinations for this game .

The one who never been on sport arena ,can be seen on TT table. The prospect of finding an empty TT room is practically nil, forcing the junta to choose all sorts of strange hours in the (often vain!)hope of finding that elusive vacancy. The facilities available are at par with the best in the World. Each hall has their individual TT rooms, in addition to the Indoor Gymnasium where leviathans fight it out on a regular basis. International approved Racquets and balls are used, not only for tournaments, but also for regular practice. But the real strength of IITK in TT lies in the quality of its players. IITK specifically coaches the interested students especially the first yearites as part of their regular course. So each year we have a team of players who are winners at state and national levels.
