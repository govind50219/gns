Basketball


Basketball is the fastest game on the planet. Apart from speed and skill, the nature of the sport demands a person who is tough and ready to give his sweat and blood on the court. However, contrary to popular perception, basketball is not limited to the big hunks. It’s a game of speed, and all it needs is someone who can fly.

Although it provides ample opportunity for showcasing individual abilities, at its heart basketball is a team game. Basketball looks most beautiful when instead of seeing five individuals on a team, one sees a machine made up of five components designed to deliver the knock-out punch. A set of five well-coordinated players can do much more damage than a team with two or three extraordinary players. Here there are no defenders and no strikers. Everyone in the team has to be good everything.
